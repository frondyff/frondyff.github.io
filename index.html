How I Work

A structured approach from problem to insight.

1

Understand the business problem

2

Structure messy data

Transform notes, documents, and raw data into usable signals through cleaning and feature work.

3

Build the system

Use analytics, AI, dashboards, and automation workflows to support clear action.

Communicate insight

Make the output legible for business users, not only technical audiences.
